At Nomada Ventures LLC ("Nomad Ventures"), we are committed to delivering high-quality digital marketing services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued.

1. Custom Service Projects

Due to the custom nature of our services, the following refund terms apply:

Before Project Commencement

  • Full refund of deposit if cancellation occurs before work begins
  • Refund request must be submitted in writing within 48 hours of payment

After Project Commencement

  • Refunds are prorated based on work completed
  • Client receives all deliverables completed up to cancellation date
  • Deposits are generally non-refundable once work has begun

2. Subscription Services

For ongoing subscription-based services:

  • Cancel anytime before the next billing cycle with no additional charges
  • No refunds for partial months or unused time
  • Access continues until end of current billing period

3. Digital Products

For downloadable digital products:

  • All sales are final once product is accessed or downloaded
  • No refunds due to the instant-access nature of digital goods
  • Technical issues will be addressed through support

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • Non-delivery: If we fail to deliver agreed-upon services
  • Material defects: If deliverables significantly deviate from specifications
  • Duplicate charges: If payment was processed more than once

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Third-party software, domains, or hosting purchased on client's behalf
  • Rush fees or expedited service charges
  • Consultation time and strategy sessions already delivered
  • Services completed and approved by the client

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at contact@nomadventures.com
  • Include your order/invoice number
  • Provide a detailed explanation of your request
  • Requests are reviewed within 5-7 business days

7. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days
  • Bank processing may add additional time

8. Disputes

We encourage clients to contact us directly before initiating chargebacks. Chargebacks result in additional fees and may affect future service eligibility. Most issues can be resolved through direct communication.
