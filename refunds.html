Thank you for choosing Nomada Ventures LLC, doing business as "Nomad Ventures." We stand behind the quality of our digital products and services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on purchases made through our website.

1. Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products (courses, training materials, templates, and downloadable content), all sales are generally considered final once access has been granted or content has been delivered. However, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase.

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee: If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund within 30 days of your purchase date, provided you have not accessed more than 25% of the digital content.

2. Consulting & Custom Services

For consulting services and custom project work:

  • Before Work Begins: Full refund available if requested before project commencement
  • Work in Progress: Partial refunds may be available based on work completed, at our discretion
  • Completed Projects: No refunds available after project delivery and approval

3. Subscription Services

For recurring subscription-based services:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting us
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
  • We do not provide partial refunds for unused portions of a subscription period
  • Access continues until the end of your paid period

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at contact@nomadventures.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  • Include your full name and the email address used for the purchase
  • Provide your order number or transaction ID
  • Briefly explain the reason for your refund request

5. Refund Processing

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will review your request within 3-5 business days
  • If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method
  • Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your financial institution
  • You will receive email confirmation once your refund has been processed

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Digital products that have been fully downloaded or extensively accessed
  • Customized services or products created specifically for you
  • Purchases made more than 30 days prior to the refund request
  • Products or services obtained through promotional pricing or special offers (unless otherwise stated)

7. Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us first before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company. We are committed to resolving any issues fairly and promptly. Initiating a chargeback without first attempting to resolve the issue with us may result in termination of your account and access to our services.
